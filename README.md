The goal of this project is to explore patient flow, staffing, and service efficiency to provide actionable insights to improve hospital performance. 
Datasets used: 1. Patients (patient's information) 
               2. Staff (Staff's information)
     3. Services Weekly (Weekly availability of beds, patient satisfaction) 
                4. Staff schedule 

This project is organized in the way: 
   1. Loading datasets 
       1.1: Exploring & Cleaning the datasets
       1.2: Summary if the cleaning section
  2. Analysis and Visualizations
      2.1: Patient Satisfaction by Service + Visualisation & Interpretation 
      2.2: Bed Occupancy Rate by Week + Interpretation
      2.3: Patients Requested V/S Admitted by Month+ Visualisation & Interpretation
     2.4: Staff Attendance and Workload Trends+ Visualization & Interpretation
